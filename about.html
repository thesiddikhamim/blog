About Md Abu Bakkar Siddik Hamim

An intellectual explorer in the realms of Economics, Philosophy, and Politics.

Welcome to my personal blog. This space serves as a digital journal where I process ideas, analyze global trends, and reflect on the timeless questions of human existence. My writing is focused on creating a calm, deliberate dialogue between traditional wisdom and modern challenges.

Interests & Focus Areas

  • Economics

    Macroeconomic policy, the evolution of monetary systems, and sustainable growth in a changing world.

  • Philosophy

    Stoicism, Existentialism, and the intersection of ethics and technology in the 21st century.

  • Politics

    Geopolitical shifts, democratic resilience, and the power of ideas in shaping international relations.

  • Tech

    The philosophical implications of AI and the profound impact of semiconductors on global power dynamics.

I believe that in an age of rapid consumption and shallow discourse, taking the time to think deeply and write carefully is a radical act. I hope these articles encourage you to pause, reflect, and engage with the world more thoughtfully.
